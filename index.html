Biography

Maximilian Vogler is an acclaimed German tenor recognized for his exceptional artistry in both opera and concert repertoire. His performances are characterized by a unique combination of technical precision and deeply expressive interpretation.

Specializing in baroque and classical works, Vogler has established himself as a sought-after interpreter of J.S. Bach's sacred music, particularly the passions and cantatas. His "balsamically sensitive tenor" has been praised by critics across Europe.

He regularly collaborates with renowned orchestras and ensembles including the Deutsche Kammerphilharmonie Bremen, B'Rock Orchestra, and Kammerchor Stuttgart, performing at prestigious venues throughout Europe.
